Things to Bring on the Day of Donation

  • A valid ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)
  • Wear a short-sleeved shirt or one that can be rolled up easily
  • A water bottle to stay hydrated
  • A light snack for after your donation
  • Any medications you are currently taking
  • Your donor card (if you have one)

Blood Donation Tips

Before Donation

  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water.
  • Eat a healthy meal: Avoid fatty foods.
  • Get plenty of sleep: Aim for at least 8 hours.
  • Wear comfortable clothing: Short sleeves are best.

After Donation

  • Rest for at least 15 minutes.
  • Drink plenty of fluids.
  • Avoid strenuous activities for the rest of the day.
  • Notify staff if you feel unwell.
